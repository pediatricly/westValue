How does westValue work?

Simple! 3 steps and a short survey:

  • Step 1 - Right Resident.
    • The resident may have given you a link straight to their custom "portal".
    • If not, you can select any resident at the Resident Directory.
  • Step 2 - Right Rotation
    • From that resident's custom "portal", just select your rotation.
    • westValue tries to make it easy by looking up the resident's schedule in Amion, but you can choose any from the list.
  • Step 3 - Pick an EPA
    • Given the rotation, westValue recommends a few EPAs common on that rotation.
    • However, you can choose any EPA you think appropriate. (Among the EPAs for which we have surveys ready - more will be available over time.)
  • A Short Survey
    • Click the EPA & you're whisked to a customized Qualtrics survey. Seriously, it's short - an intro page + one short page of questions.

And that's it! Computers are magic!

What is the deal with EPAs?

The new, coolest things in medical training feedback are Entrustable Professional Activities (EPAs).

17 EPAs cover broad domains of pediatrics. E.g., EPA #3 is "care of the well newborn".

westValue takes EPAs as the organizing framework for feedback. Instead of long, vague evals about trainees in general or whole rotations, westValue uses very short Qualtrics surveys to collect feedback on specific EPAs.

We hope these short surveys will provide a greater volume of more specific feedback. The idea is that trainees gradually learn to be entrusted to perform these EPAs independently, and westValue tracks their learning.
