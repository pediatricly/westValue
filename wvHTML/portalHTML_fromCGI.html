What's an EPA?

The new, coolest things in medical training feedback are Entrustable Professional Activities (EPAs). 17 EPAs cover broad domains of pediatrics. E.g., EPA #3 is "care of the well newborn". The idea is that trainees gradually learn to be entrusted with all these activities independently. westValue uses very short Qualtrics surveys to track trainee progress.
